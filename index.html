Have the work week you always wanted -- just without the bad wifi.

Located in the beautiful mountains of Asheville, North Carolina. Endless mounain views. Literally.

12 private rooms, each with its own bathroom. Two conference rooms. 4 smaller breakout rooms. A huge wraparound porch for taking your meetings outside.

An on-site chef, cooking you breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Want something custom done? Just ask!

An on-site obstacle course. Two private lakes with beaches. Miles of hiking trails. Multiple fire pits. You may be so busy outside that you don't have time for meetings. And we're ok with that.

Concierge who is there to assist you with anything you need during your stay. You can rest, we'll take care of everything for you.

Need wifi for a meeting? We've got it. But we change the password every night at midnight. We do want you to "unplug", after all.
